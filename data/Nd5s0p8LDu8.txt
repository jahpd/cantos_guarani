Música Mbyá Guarani #2 - Jaguota Javy

Enviado em 14 de nov de 2010

Jaguota Javy means "let's walk togetherl" in Guaraní.  This is the second in a series of recordings of the Morinigo family in aldea Katu-Pyry near San Ignacio, Misiones, Argentina.  All are new songs written over the past three years by the community.  Explore this project at The Ethnomusicologist:  http://www.ethnomusico.com
